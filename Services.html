Our Services

Book Sales

  • New & Used Books: A wide range of titles from fiction to educational, self-help to biographies.
  • Academic Materials: School and college textbooks, study guides, and exam prep resources.
  • Book Orders: Special order services for hard-to-find or international books.
  • Local Author Shelf: A dedicated section promoting books by South African writers.
  • Community Discounts: Special offers for students and teachers.

Writing Services

Our writing services are designed to support students, professionals, and businesses with high-quality content tailored to their goals.

  • Academic Writing: Essays, research papers, assignments, and proofreading.
  • Business Writing: CVs, cover letters, business profiles, proposals, and reports.
  • Creative Projects: Ghostwriting, poetry, short stories, and editing services.
  • Editing & Proofreading: Detailed grammar, structure, and clarity improvements for any written work.
  • Custom Projects: We also take on unique writing requests—just ask!

Web Development

We offer basic web development services for individuals and small businesses looking to establish a digital presence.

  • Portfolio Websites: Build and deploy clean, modern personal or business portfolios.
  • Business Sites: Affordable business websites for showcasing products, services, or contact info.
  • HTML/CSS/JS Support: Assistance with front-end web development tasks and school assignments.
  • Domain & Hosting Guidance: Help with choosing and setting up hosting for your site.

IT Diploma–Related Services

For students pursuing Information Technology qualifications, we offer academic assistance and support services tailored to diploma modules.

  • Assignment Help: Get help with coding, network concepts, and systems modules.
  • Exam Preparation: Study guides, summaries, and mock quizzes based on IT diploma curriculum.
  • Project Support: Front-end projects, database connectivity, and system design assistance.
  • CV & Portfolio Prep: Help compiling your student portfolio and career documents.

Whether you're preparing for exams, building a site, or launching your writing project—Morgets Bookshop is here to help.
